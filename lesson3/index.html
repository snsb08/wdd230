Site Purpose

This website provides information on how to become a member of the North Palm Beach Chamber of Commerce. Members can promote their business, network, attend workshops, and participate and numerous events that help to keep our town appealing to residents and visitors. Visitors can also browse the website to find fun things to do in the community and learn more about North Palm Beach.
