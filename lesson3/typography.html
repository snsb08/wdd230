Typography

Mochiy Pop P One

I chose the Mochiy Pop P One font because it is unique and bold. I am using it for the headings, mostly h1 and h2 levels of headings.

Arial Narrow

For the rest of the text, I chose Arial Narrow because it is easy to read and accesive in most browsers.

I am using the color black for the text to keep a clean design and not compete with the colorful accent colors.
