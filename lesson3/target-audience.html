Target Audience

The target audience for this website are the business owners and professionals of the town of North Palm Beach.

Personas

Daniel Bustillo

38 year-old married with children, college graduate, and business owner. He owns a language school of Spanish and English. He accesses the site for network opportunities as his business brings clients mainly by word of mouth.

Scenario questions that Daniel might ask:

  1. Is there a fee to become a member?
  2. What are the benefits of membership?
  3. What events are currently happening?
  4. What other businesses are active members of this group?
  5. Are there other businesses in my industry in this area?

Katya Jacobs

19 year-old high-school graduate. She is enrolled in the Community College. She is driven and ready to start entering the professional market. She wants to get involved in community volunteer opportunities to enhance her resume. She is also new to the area and wants to learn more about fun things to do around town.

Scenario questions that Katya might ask:

  1. Is there a student membership?
  2. What kind of career workshops are offered?
  3. How often do they have events?
  4. What are the recreation facilities and programs in town?
  5. Do they have job postings?
